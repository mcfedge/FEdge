# Alamo ACE 2024 Organizational Smart Sheets Report

## Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive set of "smart sheets" for the key organizations and leaders attending Alamo ACE in early December. Each smart sheet distills essential information—mission, leadership, key programs, and capabilities—into a concise, actionable format. The goal is to equip the team with a unified, in-depth understanding of each organization’s strategic priorities, modernization efforts, and industry partnerships, ensuring effective engagement and collaboration at the event.

## Introduction

The purpose of this report is to provide the Alamo ACE team with a set of tailored smart sheets for PEO C3BM, HN, NC3, and ES. These organizations are central to the Department of Defense’s modernization and operational effectiveness, each contributing unique capabilities in command and control, nuclear deterrence, and electronic systems. The report synthesizes available data, highlights leadership and programmatic priorities, and identifies areas for further inquiry where information is limited. This approach ensures the team is well-prepared to engage with stakeholders, foster collaboration, and advance mission objectives at Alamo ACE.

## PEO C3BM Smart Sheet

**Program Executive Office for Command, Control, Battle Management (PEO C3BM)**

### Mission

Deliver next-generation command and control (NGC2) capabilities for the Army, integrating advanced technologies to ensure decision dominance and operational superiority. PEO C3BM’s mission aligns with DoD’s Joint All-Domain Command and Control (CJADC2) vision, focusing on interoperability, data-driven decision-making, and operational agility ([NGC2 Industry Day Presentation.pdf](https://gabrielroboblocker.atlassian.net/wiki/pages/viewpageattachments.action?pageId=2682290218&preview=%2F2682290218%2F2681831679%2FNGC2+Industry+Day+Presentation.pdf "https://gabrielroboblocker.atlassian.net/wiki/pages/viewpageattachments.action?pageId=2682290218&preview=%2F2682290218%2F2681831679%2FNGC2+Industry+Day+Presentation.pdf")).

### Key Leaders

- MG Cropsey
    
- Mr. Gordon Kordyak (retiring January)
    

### Key Programs/Capabilities

|Program/Capability|Description|
|---|---|
|**NGC2**|Integrates AI, quantum computing, data mesh, and zero trust to create a unified C2 framework|
|**Project Convergence**|Platform for testing/integrating new tech and concepts into Army C2; emphasizes joint/coalition experimentation|
|**NetModX**|Modernizes tactical networks for resilience, scalability, and security (Zero Trust, hybrid cloud)|
|**SBIRs & Competitions**|Engages small businesses for innovation and capability gap solutions|
|**Deliberate Pilots & Continuous Integration**|Rapidly tests, refines, and deploys emerging capabilities|
|**Persistent Experimentation**|Adopts "-as-a-Service" models for infrastructure/communications; explores new operational concepts|
|**Portfolio Approach**|Leverages DoD/Joint investments in CJADC2; continuous transformation and modernization|

### Modernization Focus

- Breaking down C2 silos for seamless data flow
    
- Persistent experimentation and rapid integration
    
- Industry collaboration for innovation
    
- Emphasis on data-centric, scalable, and resilient architectures
    

### Industry Partners

- **Cubic Corporation**: Advanced comms solutions ([2-6SEP24](https://gabrielroboblocker.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/~712020651394fb455b447ead29be491a8ac549/pages/2681897143/2-6SEP24))
    
- **Thunder**: Hardware/services for NGC2 ([18-22AUG25](https://gabrielroboblocker.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/~712020651394fb455b447ead29be491a8ac549/pages/3172040723/18-22AUG25))
    
- **Isidore**: Post-quantum, zero-trust tactical network solutions ([28JUL-1AUG25](https://gabrielroboblocker.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/~712020651394fb455b447ead29be491a8ac549/pages/3137798145/28JUL-1AUG25))
    
- **Forward Edge AI**: AI/logistics modernization ([2025-08-22 Meeting Notes](https://gabrielroboblocker.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DTAM/pages/3182428184/2025-08-22+Meeting+Notes))

## NC3 Smart Sheet

**Nuclear Command, Control, and Communications (NC3)**

### Mission

Provide robust, survivable, and secure communications between the President, Secretary of Defense, and nuclear forces, supporting nuclear force planning, situation monitoring, force management, force direction, and decision-making ([SACCS_Industry_Days_Immersion--Brief_1.pptx](https://gabrielroboblocker.atlassian.net/wiki/pages/viewpageattachments.action?pageId=2626617346&preview=%2F2626617346%2F2628124692%2FSACCS_Industry_Days_Immersion--Brief_1.pptx "https://gabrielroboblocker.atlassian.net/wiki/pages/viewpageattachments.action?pageId=2626617346&preview=%2F2626617346%2F2628124692%2FSACCS_Industry_Days_Immersion--Brief_1.pptx")).

### Key Leaders

- Mr. Scott Hardiman
    
- Mr. Mike Clark (Head of Engineering)
    

### Key Programs/Capabilities

|Program/Capability|Description|
|---|---|
