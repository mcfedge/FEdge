
## **Mission:**  
Forward Edge-AI delivers secure, scalable, and mission-aligned automation solutions for government and enterprise clients. Their focus is on using AI, Robotic Process Automation (RPA), and digital integration to solve complex operational challenges—especially in public sector, defense, and regulated industries.

**Core Capabilities:**

- AI-powered automation (RPA, ML, data pipelines)
    
- Integration with legacy systems (PRISM, WebTA, IFMIS, etc.)
    
- Document understanding, workflow automation, and knowledge management
    
- Secure, compliant, and rapid deployment (FedRAMP, IL5 environments)
    
- Flexible business models (SBIR, fixed-price, cost-reimbursement)

### **1. Preboarding / Initial Engagement**

- **Partner/Client Selection:** Identify fit and mission needs.
    
- **Welcome & Portal Setup:** Provide access to onboarding resources and communication channels.
    

---

### **2. Planning & Discovery**

- **Needs Assessment:** Understand client personas, pain points, and desired outcomes.
    
- **Tailored Journey:** Map out a custom onboarding and delivery plan.
    
- **Kickoff Meeting:** Align on goals, timelines, and responsibilities.
    

---

### **3. Roadmap & Requirements**

- **Define Milestones:** Set clear process steps, deliverables, and checkpoints.
    
- **Document Requirements:** Gather and formalize requirements (Statement of Work, SRS, Use Cases).
    
- **Assign Roles:** Clarify responsibilities for both Forward Edge and client teams.
    

---

### **4. Training & Enablement**

- **Product/Process Training:** Deliver training modules, certifications, and resources (via Docebo or similar platforms).
    
- **Knowledge Transfer:** Ensure client teams understand tools, dashboards, and automation workflows.
    

---

### **5. Solution Design & Development**

- **Technical Scoping:** Detailed design of automation, AI models, and integration points.
    
- **Agile Delivery:** Iterative development with regular check-ins and feedback loops.
    
- **Artifact Creation:** Develop reusable automation artifacts, templates, and documentation.
    

---

### **6. Deployment & Integration**

- **System Integration:** Connect automation to client’s legacy and cloud systems.
    
- **Testing & Validation:** Run pilots, validate outcomes, and refine as needed.
    
- **Go-Live:** Full deployment with support for hyper care and rapid response.
    

---

### **7. Performance Tracking & Continuous Improvement**

- **Monitor KPIs:** Track efficiency gains, cost savings, and user adoption.
    
- **Ongoing Engagement:** Regular check-ins, newsletters, webinars, and business planning sessions.
    
- **Feedback Loops:** Use analytics and client feedback to optimize processes and update training/resources.
    

---

### **8. Completion & Scale**

- **Project Closeout:** Finalize deliverables, document outcomes, and transition to steady-state support.
    
- **Scale Up:** Option to expand automation to new workflows or departments.
    
- **Continuous Learning:** Keep resources and training available for ongoing improvement.
